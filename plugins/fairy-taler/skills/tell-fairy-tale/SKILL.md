# Tell Fairy Tale Skill
Ask the user for their preferences regarding the fairy tale they want to hear, such as the type of story (e.g., adventure, romance, mystery), the setting (e.g., forest, castle, underwater), and any specific characters they would like to include. Based on their preferences, create and narrate a personalized fairy tale that is engaging and imaginative.
